How?

Just invite it, use -configure to set the channel where you can dispatch memes to unsuspecting servers and have messages wash on your shore. Distributing bottles is a matter of prefixes: use > message to send out a message, -> message to reply to the previous message, and ->> message to reply to the last received bottle. The only strictly enforced rule is sfw-only.
